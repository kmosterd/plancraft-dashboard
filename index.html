KPI Scorecards — Each card shows the FY24 value, YoY change vs FY23 (YoY% = (FY24 − FY23) ÷ FY23 × 100), and target attainment (Attainment% = Actual ÷ Target × 100). Traffic lights: ● ≥98% of target, ● 90–98%, ● <90%. For Spend, lower is better (inverted logic). ROAS = Revenue ÷ Spend.
Automated Insights Engine — The insights below are not hardcoded — they are dynamically generated from the filtered dataset on every interaction. The engine runs 6 analysis rules: 1) Highest ROAS channel: ranks all channels by Revenue ÷ Spend, surfaces the #1 performer. 2) Budget vs Revenue gap: compares each channel's % of total spend vs % of total revenue — flags channels where revenue share exceeds spend share by >5pp (= scaling opportunity) or vice versa (= reallocation candidate). 3) MRR growth leader: ranks channels by MRR YoY% to identify which channel builds the most recurring revenue. 4) Best/worst campaign: ranks all individual campaigns by ROAS to surface standout performers and underperformers. 5) Rising CAC risk: flags channels where CAC YoY% exceeds +5% — acquisition is getting more expensive. 6) Lowest ROAS channel: identifies the weakest returning channel for budget review. All insights update instantly when you change filters (country, channel, period). In production: thresholds would be configurable and insights could trigger Slack/email alerts.

MRR per Deal & CAC Payback

Customer quality: higher MRR/deal = faster payback
Bars: MRR/Deal = Total MRR ÷ Deals Won — the average monthly recurring revenue per acquired customer. Higher = more valuable customers. Line: CAC Payback = CAC ÷ MRR/Deal — number of months to recover the acquisition cost from recurring revenue alone. Data Sources & Methodology

  • Source data: 36 campaign rows across 3 countries (Germany, Austria, France), 5 channels (Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Organic Search, Referral), 12 unique campaigns. Original dataset provided as CSV with FY24 annual totals.
  • FY23 (Last Year) data: Generated by applying channel-specific growth factors to FY24 data (reverse-engineering). Growth rates reflect realistic channel maturity: SEO +20–33% YoY (strong organic growth), Paid Search +12–22% (market expansion), Meta +8–15% (scaling), LinkedIn +4–8% (newer channel), Referral +10–15%. In production: pulled from data warehouse / BI tool.
  • FY24 Targets: Per-campaign targets derived from annual planning assumptions. Ambitious for scaling channels (LinkedIn +22%, Display +15%), conservative for mature channels (SEO -6–10% budget targets with higher efficiency expectations). In production: set via OKR planning process.
  • Monthly distribution: Annual totals are distributed across 12 months using construction-industry seasonality weights (peak: Mar–Jun at 8.5–9.5%, Sep–Nov at 9.0–9.5%; low: Jul–Aug at 7.0–7.5%, Dec–Jan at 6.5–7.0%). Deterministic variation ensures consistency on page reload.
  • Territory: Sub-regions assigned per country (Germany: Bayern/NRW/Berlin-Brandenburg, Austria: Wien/Westösterreich, France: Île-de-France/Sud-Est). In production: derived from campaign targeting settings or CRM data.
  • Campaign Type: Strategic classification derived from campaign naming: Brand (branded search), Non-Brand Search, Retargeting (display + social remarketing), Lead Generation, Prospecting (lookalike), Industry Targeting (LinkedIn), Content (SEO), Referral (partnerships).

Key Formula Reference

  • CTR: Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100 — measures ad/content relevance and click appeal
  • CPM: (Spend ÷ Impressions) × 1,000 — cost to reach 1,000 people
  • CPL: Spend ÷ MQLs — cost per marketing-qualified lead
  • CAC: Spend ÷ Deals Won — total cost to acquire one paying customer
  • CVR (Click→MQL): MQLs ÷ Clicks × 100 — landing page / form effectiveness
  • CVR (MQL→Deal): Deals ÷ MQLs × 100 — lead quality and sales effectiveness
  • ROAS: Revenue ÷ Spend — revenue generated per euro invested
  • MRR/Deal: Total MRR ÷ Deals Won — average monthly recurring revenue per customer
  • CAC Payback: CAC ÷ MRR/Deal — months to recover acquisition cost from recurring revenue
  • Net MRR Bridge: Base MRR (85% retention of FY23) + New MRR per channel − Churn (10% of FY23 MRR)
  • YoY %: (FY24 − FY23) ÷ FY23 × 100 — year-over-year growth rate
  • Target Attainment: Actual ÷ Target × 100 — percentage of goal achieved
  • Δ in pp: Percentage point difference (e.g. 12.1% − 10.0% = +2.1pp) — used for conversion rate comparisons
