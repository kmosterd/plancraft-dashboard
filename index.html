Assumptions & Methodology

  • LY (FY23): Simulated growth rates per channel (SEO ~25-33%, Paid ~10-22%, LinkedIn ~5-8%). Production: data warehouse.
  • Targets: Per-channel targets. SEO exceeded; LinkedIn missed. Production: annual planning OKRs.
  • Monthly: Annual totals distributed with construction-industry seasonality + deterministic variation.
  • Net MRR: New campaign MRR minus ~10% estimated churn. Production: billing/subscription data.
  • CPM: (Spend ÷ Impressions) × 1,000. For Organic/Referral: content/partnership cost per 1K reach.
  • CAC Payback: CAC ÷ MRR/Deal — months to recover acquisition cost from recurring revenue.
  • Heatmap ROAS: Revenue ÷ Spend per country×channel combination.
  • Insights: Auto-generated from data. In production: configurable thresholds + Slack alerts.
